Nepsis

Nepsis helps you build intentional screen time habits using Apple's Screen Time APIs — local-first, private, and with no account required.

What you can do with Nepsis

  • Daily Limits — set a time budget for distracting apps
  • Schedules — block apps automatically during focus windows
  • Just Block — block distracting apps immediately, on demand
  • Protection levels — Easy, Focused, Committed, or Strict, so friction matches your commitment
  • Physical & Mindful Challenges — optional on-device challenges before unblocking
  • Insights & Weekly Reports — local usage summaries, never uploaded
  • Custom Block Screen — personalize what you see when an app is blocked
  • Mindful Open — add Pre-Open Friction via Shortcuts before opening distracting apps

Nepsis is local-first: there's no Nepsis account, your usage data stays on your device, and iCloud (when available) syncs only rule and settings metadata — never your Screen Time app selections or usage history.
