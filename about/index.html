About Nepsis

Last Updated: July 11, 2026

Nepsis is a screen time control app for iPhone and iPad. It helps you set intentional limits on distracting apps using Apple's Screen Time APIs — with schedules, strict mode, and iCloud sync of rule settings across your devices when iCloud is available.

Our approach

  • Local-first: Your usage data and blocks stay on your device. No account required.
  • Privacy by design: We don't run servers to collect your Screen Time data.
  • Intentional friction: Strict mode and passcode protection when you need stronger commitment.
  • Your devices, your rules: When signed into iCloud, rule metadata stays aligned across iPhone and iPad (app selections must be re-chosen per device).
