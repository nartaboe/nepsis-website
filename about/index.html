About Nepsis

Last Updated: July 16, 2026

Nepsis is a focus and digital wellbeing app for iPhone and iPad. It helps you build intentional screen time habits using Apple's Screen Time APIs — with limits, schedules, protection levels, and iCloud sync of rule and settings metadata across your devices when iCloud is available.

What Nepsis offers

  • Daily Limits: Set a daily time budget for distracting apps.
  • Schedules: Automatically block apps during recurring focus windows.
  • Just Block: Block distracting apps immediately, without setting up a schedule.
  • Protection levels: Choose Easy, Focused, Committed, or Strict — each adding more friction before you can disable or edit a rule.
  • Physical & Mindful Challenges: Optional camera, microphone, or speech-based challenges (processed on-device) before unblocking.
  • Insights & Weekly Reports: Local usage summaries and trends, generated and stored only on your device.
  • Custom Block Screen: Personalize the appearance of the screen you see when an app is blocked.
  • Mindful Open: Add Pre-Open Friction via Shortcuts before opening apps you want to use more mindfully.

Our approach

  • Local-first: Your usage data and blocks stay on your device. No Nepsis account required.
  • Privacy by design: We don't run servers to collect your Screen Time data.
  • Intentional friction: Protection levels — up to Strict — add passcode and challenge-based friction when you need stronger commitment.
  • Your devices, your rules: When signed into iCloud, rule and settings metadata stays aligned across iPhone and iPad (Screen Time app selections must be re-chosen per device — an Apple platform limitation).
  • Premium: Optional Premium features are purchased through the App Store and managed anonymously via RevenueCat — no personal account needed.
